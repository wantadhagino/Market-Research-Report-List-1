<p><strong>The Briquetting Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Briquetting Machines</li><li>Screw Briquetting Machines</li><li>Hydraulic Briquetting Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The briquetting machines market is segmented into three main types: mechanical, screw, and hydraulic briquetting machines. Mechanical briquetting machines utilize a piston mechanism to compress raw materials into solid briquettes, typically producing high-density output. Screw briquetting machines employ a screw-type mechanism to feed and compress biomass, allowing for continuous operation and flexibility in feedstock. Hydraulic briquetting machines use hydraulic pressure to form briquettes, offering high compaction and efficiency, suitable for a variety of materials. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
