<p><p>The Spunbond Polypropylene Nonwovens market is categorized into hydrophobic and hydrophilic types. Hydrophobic PP nonwovens repel water and are ideal for applications requiring moisture resistance, such as in protective clothing and certain medical products. In contrast, hydrophilic PP nonwovens absorb moisture, making them suitable for applications like hygiene products and medical textiles where fluid management is crucial. Both types leverage the unique properties of polypropylene to meet specific performance requirements across various industries, including healthcare and consumer products.</p></p>
