<p><p>The carbide-based cermet market encompasses materials like silicon carbide, tungsten carbide, and titanium carbide, each with distinct properties and applications. Silicon carbide is known for its exceptional thermal stability and wear resistance, making it ideal for cutting tools and abrasive applications. Tungsten carbide exhibits high hardness and toughness, commonly used in mining, machining, and jewelry. Titanium carbide, lighter than tungsten, offers good wear resistance and is often utilized in cutting tools and coatings, enhancing the performance of various industrial applications.</p></p>
