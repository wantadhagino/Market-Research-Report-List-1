<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Splitter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fused Biconic Tapered Splitters</li><li>Planar Lightwave Circuit Splitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic splitter market primarily features two types: Fused Biconic Tapered Splitters (FBT) and Planar Lightwave Circuit (PLC) Splitters. FBT splitters use a fused technique to divide light signals and are suitable for short-range applications due to their compact design. In contrast, PLC splitters utilize a multi-mode planar waveguide technology, offering a more uniform splitting ratio and supporting larger networks. Both types cater to the growing demand for efficient signal distribution in telecommunication and data transmission systems.</p></p>
