<p><p>The multistage steam turbine market is categorized into three main types: condensing, back pressure, and others. Condensing turbines convert steam into water, maximizing energy efficiency by utilizing heat exchange. Back pressure turbines maintain steam pressure for industrial processes, providing direct power while facilitating heat recycling. Other types include extraction turbines, which draw steam at various pressures for process heating. Each type serves distinct applications in power generation and industrial operations, driving efficiency and energy management across sectors.</p></p>
