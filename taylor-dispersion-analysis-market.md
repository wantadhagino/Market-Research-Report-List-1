<p><p>Taylor Dispersion Analysis (TDA) encompasses two primary market types: the Taylor Discrete Model and the Taylor Continuous Model. The Discrete Model focuses on analyzing the dispersion of particles in a defined period, capturing fluctuations and variations in a discrete manner. In contrast, the Continuous Model evaluates the ongoing dispersion of particles over time, providing a more fluid perspective on how dispersive characteristics evolve. Both models are essential in understanding particle dynamics in various applications, such as material science and drug delivery.</p></p>
