<p><p>The automotive infrared thermal imaging camera market is primarily divided into two types: uncooled and cooled thermal cameras. Uncooled type cameras operate at ambient temperatures, using sensors that do not require any cooling mechanism, making them more compact and cost-effective. In contrast, cooled type cameras utilize cryogenic cooling to enhance sensitivity and accuracy, providing better image quality in low-contrast environments. Both types play crucial roles in advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS), enhancing safety by detecting obstacles and monitoring driver conditions.</p></p>
