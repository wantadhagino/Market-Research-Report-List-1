<p><p>The 2D Image Sensor market is primarily categorized into three types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor), CCD (Charge-Coupled Device), and others. CMOS sensors are favored for their low power consumption, compact design, and faster processing speeds, making them ideal for smartphones and digital cameras. CCD sensors, known for their high-quality imaging and low noise levels, are commonly used in professional photography and scientific applications. Other types include specialized sensors like the Foveon and infrared sensors, catering to niche markets.</p></p>
