<p><p>Transport Management Software (TMS) is available in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based TMS is hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, accessibility, and automatic updates, which enhances collaboration and reduces IT overhead. On-premises TMS, on the other hand, is installed locally on a company’s servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring more substantial upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Both options cater to different business needs depending on size, budget, and operational preferences.</p></p>
