<p><p>The velocity sensors market encompasses various technologies, primarily focusing on moving coil and piezoelectric sensors. Moving coil velocity sensors operate on electromagnetic principles, providing precise measurements of linear displacement velocity, commonly used in applications like vibration analysis. In contrast, piezoelectric velocity sensors leverage the piezoelectric effect, generating electric charges in response to mechanical stress, making them ideal for dynamic measurements in harsh environments. Both types cater to industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, driving advancements in vibration monitoring and condition assessment.</p></p>
