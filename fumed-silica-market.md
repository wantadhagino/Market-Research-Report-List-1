<p><p>The fumed silica market is categorized based on specific surface area measurements, known as BET (Brunauer-Emmett-Teller) values. Types include BET 100-160, which offers low surface area suitable for applications requiring moderate thickening. BET 160-210 provides enhanced thickening and reinforcement properties, while BET 210-300 serves industries needing high absorbency and viscosity control. The "Others" category encompasses specialized grades tailored for niche applications, focusing on performance attributes like hydrophobicity or enhanced flow characteristics, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
