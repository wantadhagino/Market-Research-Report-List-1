<p><p>The rubber-coated fabrics market is bifurcated into natural rubber and synthetic rubber types. Natural rubber-coated fabrics are derived from latex, offering excellent elasticity and comfort, making them suitable for applications like upholstery and protective clothing. In contrast, synthetic rubber-coated fabrics, made from materials like neoprene or silicone, provide enhanced durability, weather resistance, and chemical protection, commonly used in industrial, automotive, and outdoor gear. Both types cater to diverse industries, depending on the specific performance and application requirements.</p></p>
