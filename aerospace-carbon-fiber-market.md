<p><strong>The Aerospace Carbon Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyacrylonitrile-Based Carbon Fiber</li><li>Pitch-Based Carbon Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace carbon fiber market is primarily divided into two types: polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based carbon fiber and pitch-based carbon fiber. PAN-based carbon fiber is widely used due to its high tensile strength, low weight, and excellent stiffness, making it ideal for structural applications in aircraft. In contrast, pitch-based carbon fiber offers superior thermal and electrical conductivity, making it more suitable for specialized components, such as thermal insulation and conductive materials in aerospace applications. Each type serves distinct needs within the industry.</p></p>
