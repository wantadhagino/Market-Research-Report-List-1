<p><ul><li>Millimeter Wave Radiometer</li><li>Interferometric Correlator Radiometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airborne radiometer market is primarily categorized into two types: Millimeter Wave Radiometers and Interferometric Correlator Radiometers. Millimeter Wave Radiometers operate within the millimeter wave frequency range, enabling high-resolution atmospheric observations and remote sensing applications. They are particularly effective for studying cloud formation and precipitation. On the other hand, Interferometric Correlator Radiometers utilize advanced signal processing techniques to analyze incoming radiation from multiple sources, enhancing measurement accuracy and sensitivity, especially in scientific research and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
