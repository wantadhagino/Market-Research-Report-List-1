<p><p>The Automatic Urine Analysis System market is segmented into two main types: dry analysis and wet analysis. Dry analysis utilizes test strips or reagents that react with urine, allowing for rapid results with minimal sample preparation. In contrast, wet analysis involves the use of liquid reagents and typically requires more sophisticated equipment for processing and interpreting results. Both methods play a crucial role in diagnostics, offering efficiency and accuracy in urinary examinations for various health assessments.</p></p>
