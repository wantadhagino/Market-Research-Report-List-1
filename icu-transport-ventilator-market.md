<p><p>The ICU transport ventilator market can be categorized into three main types: electronic, pneumatic, and electro-pneumatic. Electronic ventilators utilize digital technology for precise control and monitoring of airflow, making them adaptable for various patient needs. Pneumatic ventilators rely on compressed air, offering a robust and portable solution in emergency situations, though they may lack advanced features. Electro-pneumatic ventilators combine both electronic and pneumatic elements, providing versatility and efficiency while ensuring reliable operation across diverse clinical environments.</p></p>
