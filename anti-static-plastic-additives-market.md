<p><p>The Anti-Static Plastic Additives market is divided into two main types: Coating Type and Internal Additive Type. Coating Type additives are applied as surface layers to impart anti-static properties, ensuring protection against static electricity on the surface of plastic materials. In contrast, Internal Additive Type additives are mixed into the plastic during manufacturing, providing inherent anti-static properties throughout the material. Both types are essential for reducing dust attraction and enhancing the safety and performance of plastic products across various industries.</p></p>
