<p><ul><li>Single Type</li><li>Multiple Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial aluminium gear pumps are classified into two main types: single type and multiple type. Single type gear pumps feature a single gear set that efficiently transfers fluids, making them ideal for applications requiring consistent flow rates and moderate pressure. In contrast, multiple type gear pumps consist of two or more gear sets, allowing for higher flow capacities and the ability to handle more complex fluid transfer needs. Both types are lightweight and corrosion-resistant, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
