<p><p>The thumb suspenders market encompasses products designed to provide support and immobilization for the thumb. Two primary types are splints and support straps. Splints are rigid devices that stabilize the thumb, often used for injuries or post-surgery recovery, ensuring proper alignment and limiting movement. Support straps, on the other hand, are typically adjustable and provide compression and mild support, suitable for conditions like arthritis or strain. Together, these products cater to varying needs for thumb protection and rehabilitation.</p></p>
