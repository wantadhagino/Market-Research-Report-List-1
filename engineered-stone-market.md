<p><p>The engineered stone market primarily consists of solid surface and engineered quartz stone types. Solid surface is a non-porous material made from a blend of acrylic and natural minerals, offering versatility in designs and easy maintenance. It is often used for countertops and sinks. Engineered quartz stone, on the other hand, combines natural quartz crystals with resins, providing exceptional durability and a wide range of aesthetic options. Both types serve various applications in residential and commercial spaces, appealing to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
