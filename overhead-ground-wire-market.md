<p><strong>The Overhead Ground Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The overhead ground wire market comprises two main types: central tube structures and layer stranding structures. Central tube structures feature a hollow core that allows for the integration of additional functionalities, such as fiber optic cables, while maintaining strength and lightweight design. Layer stranding structures, on the other hand, consist of multiple layers of conductive wires twisted around a central core, enhancing conductivity and flexibility. Both designs are crucial for protecting transmission lines from electrical surges and environmental hazards.</p></p>
