<p><p>Non-skid coatings are essential for enhancing safety on surfaces where slip risks exist. The market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based coatings. Water-based non-skid coatings are environmentally friendly, easy to clean, and offer quick drying times, making them ideal for indoor applications. Solvent-based non-skid coatings provide exceptional durability and resistance to harsh conditions, making them suitable for outdoor and industrial environments. Both types cater to different needs, ensuring safety across various surfaces and settings.</p></p>
