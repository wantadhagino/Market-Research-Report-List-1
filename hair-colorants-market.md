<p><p>The hair colorants market comprises three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent. Temporary hair colorants provide a short-term color change, lasting only until the next wash. Semi-permanent options offer a moderate-duration color, typically lasting several washes without harsh chemicals, making them less damaging. Permanent hair colorants penetrate the hair shaft for a long-lasting effect, requiring regrowth maintenance as the natural hair color reemerges. Each type caters to diverse consumer needs, from experimentation to long-term color solutions.</p></p>
