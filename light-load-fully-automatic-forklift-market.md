<p><p>The light load fully automatic forklift market can be categorized into two main types: fork and piggyback forklifts. Fork forklifts are designed for efficiently lifting and transporting light materials within warehouses or manufacturing facilities, featuring an integrated fork system. Piggyback forklifts, on the other hand, are equipped with an additional platform for carrying smaller loads on a trailer, enhancing versatility in logistics and material handling. Both types focus on optimizing space and improving operational efficiency in various industrial settings.</p></p>
