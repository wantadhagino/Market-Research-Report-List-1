<p><p>The jet hand dryer market can be categorized into two main types: hand-in and hand-under models. Hand-in dryers require users to insert their hands into an enclosed chamber where high-velocity air effectively dries them. In contrast, hand-under dryers allow users to position their hands beneath a spout, with air blowing directly downwards, facilitating a more open drying experience. Both types aim to promote hygiene and reduce paper towel waste, appealing to various commercial, public, and residential settings.</p></p>
