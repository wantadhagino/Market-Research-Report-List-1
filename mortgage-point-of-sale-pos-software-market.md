<p><p>Mortgage Point of Sale (POS) software is categorized into two main market types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises. Cloud-Based POS software is hosted on remote servers, enabling lenders and borrowers to access the system from anywhere, ensuring flexibility and scalability with lower upfront costs. Conversely, On-Premises software is installed locally on a lender's hardware, offering greater control over data security and customization but requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Both types cater to varying preferences and operational needs in the mortgage industry.</p></p>
