<p><strong>The Foundry Sand Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Green Sand (Clay Sand)</li><li>Water Glass Sand (Sodium Silicate Sand)</li><li>Resin Sand (Phenolic</li><li>Furan)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foundry sand market comprises various types, each serving different casting needs. Green sand, a clay-based mixture, is cost-effective and recyclable, ideal for ferrous metal castings. Water glass sand, using sodium silicate, enhances strength and requires less binder, making it suitable for precision molds. Resin sand, primarily phenolic and furan types, offers exceptional finish and durability, supporting complex designs and high-precision applications. Each type contributes uniquely to efficiency, quality, and versatility in metal casting processes.</p></p>
