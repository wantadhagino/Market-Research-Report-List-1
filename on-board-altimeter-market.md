<p><p>The on-board altimeter market encompasses various technologies for altitude measurement. Barometric altimeters use atmospheric pressure changes to determine altitude. Radio altimeters employ radar waves to provide precise altitude measurement above ground level, particularly for aircraft. GNSS altimeters utilize signals from satellite systems like GPS and Galileo for accurate positioning. Laser altimeters use laser beams to measure distances, providing high-resolution altitude data. Additionally, the market includes specialized altimeters for various applications, such as military, UAV, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
