<p><p>The industrial washers market includes various cleaning methods, primarily immersion cleaning and ultrasonic cleaning. Immersion cleaning involves submerging parts in a cleaning solution, utilizing chemical reactions to remove contaminants effectively. In contrast, ultrasonic cleaning employs high-frequency sound waves to create microscopic bubbles in the cleaning solution, which implode and dislodge dirt and debris from surfaces. Both methods are widely used across industries for their efficiency, enabling thorough cleaning of complex parts and improving overall operational cleanliness.</p></p>
