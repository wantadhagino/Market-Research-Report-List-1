<p><p>The marine life jacket market comprises two main types: buoyant material filled life jackets and inflatable life jackets. Buoyant material filled jackets are made from foam or similar materials that provide inherent buoyancy, making them reliable even when wet. Inflatable life jackets, on the other hand, utilize compressed gas to rapidly inflate when activated, offering compact storage and lightweight design. Both types serve the critical purpose of ensuring safety on water, appealing to various user preferences and activities.</p></p>
