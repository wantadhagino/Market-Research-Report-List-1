<p><p>The PM2.5 air quality detector market encompasses various technologies for measuring particulate matter. The TEOM (Tapered Element Oscillating Microbalance) monitor provides real-time measurements by collecting particles on a filter and weighing them. The Beta Attenuation Monitor uses beta radiation to assess particle concentration based on the attenuation of particles in the path of the beam. Additionally, other types may include optical monitors and gravimetric methods, broadening the range of options for air quality monitoring solutions.</p></p>
