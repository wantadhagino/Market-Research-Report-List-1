<p><p>Digital Classroom Management Software can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions are hosted online, allowing for easy access from multiple devices and locations, promoting collaboration and real-time updates. Both types cater to diverse educational needs and institutional preferences.</p></p>
