<p><p>Veneer-faced panels for furniture come primarily in two market types: particleboard faced and MDF (Medium Density Fiberboard) faced panels. Particleboard faced panels are constructed using wood chips and adhesives, providing a cost-effective option with decent durability and surface finish. In contrast, MDF faced panels are made from finer wood fibers, resulting in a smoother, denser surface ideal for detailed finishes and intricate designs. Both types offer versatility in furniture design, catering to various aesthetic and functional requirements.</p></p>
