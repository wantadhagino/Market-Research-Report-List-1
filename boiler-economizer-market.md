<p><strong>The Boiler Economizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steaming Economizer</li><li>Nonsteaming Economizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The boiler economizer market is primarily divided into two types: steaming economizers and non-steaming economizers. Steaming economizers are designed to preheat feedwater using flue gases, enhancing steam generation efficiency in power plants. In contrast, non-steaming economizers operate by recovering waste heat from flue gases, thereby increasing thermal efficiency without generating steam. Both types are crucial for energy conservation, reducing operational costs, and minimizing emissions, making them essential components in various industrial applications aiming for sustainability and efficiency.</p></p>
