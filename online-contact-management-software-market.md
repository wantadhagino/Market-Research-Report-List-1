<p><p>Online contact management software is typically categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessible via the internet, offering scalability, remote access, and automatic updates, making it ideal for businesses with distributed teams. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed locally on a company's servers, providing complete control over data and security but requiring more maintenance and upfront investment. Both options cater to different business needs and preferences.</p></p>
