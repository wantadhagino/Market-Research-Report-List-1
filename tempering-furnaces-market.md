<p><p>The tempering furnace market is segmented into two main types: batch type and continuous type. Batch type furnaces process materials in individual lots, allowing for flexibility and control over specific heating cycles, making them ideal for smaller operations or specialized applications. In contrast, continuous type furnaces maintain a constant flow of materials, providing higher efficiency and productivity for large-scale operations. Each type serves distinct operational needs, catering to varying production volumes and product requirements in industries such as metalworking and ceramics.</p></p>
