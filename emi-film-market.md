<p><strong>The EMI Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal-mesh Film</li><li>Coating Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EMI film markets comprise two primary types: metal-mesh films and coating films. Metal-mesh films utilize conductive mesh patterns to effectively block electromagnetic interference (EMI) while maintaining transparency and flexibility, making them suitable for applications in displays and smart devices. Coating films, on the other hand, involve applying a conductive layer onto surfaces to provide EMI shielding. These films are versatile and can be used on various substrates, enhancing their functionality in electronics and communication devices by minimizing signal disruption.</p></p>
