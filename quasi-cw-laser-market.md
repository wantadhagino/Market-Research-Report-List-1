<p><p>The quasi-CW laser market is primarily divided into two types: optical fiber and solid-state lasers. Optical fiber quasi-CW lasers utilize optical fibers to generate and transmit laser light, offering high efficiency and compactness, making them suitable for telecommunications and medical applications. On the other hand, solid-state quasi-CW lasers use solid-state materials like crystals or semiconductors, providing high power outputs and robustness, often used in industrial cutting, welding, and defense sectors. Both types enable precise applications with varying advantages.</p></p>
